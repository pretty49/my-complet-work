About Me

Halima Masud, a multi-talented creative force from Edo State, Nigeria, blends the worlds of technology and traditional craftsmanship. As a skilled front-end developer, I build beautiful, responsive websites that provide seamless digital experiences. My passion for storytelling extends beyond code into the realm of poetry, where I weave words with emotion and insight. With a deep appreciation for bespoke artistry, I also design and produce unique handmade wear for children under my brand, The Digital Loom.

At just 23, I am a remarkable example of a new generation of Nigerian creatives who masterfully merge digital innovation with heartfelt, tangible art.
